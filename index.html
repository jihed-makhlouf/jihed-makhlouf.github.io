Biography

I am a software engineer and a postdoctoral researcher working at Kyushu University and Osaka Prefecture University in Japan. My main research area is educational technology, educational data mining, and the application of artificial intelligence to enhance education. For more details about my research projects, please check the Projects section.

Besides my work as a researcher, I am a full-stack software engineer and I have been working on freelance projects and remote consultancy missions for different clients mainly in France and KSA. For more details about my services, please head toward the Experience page.
