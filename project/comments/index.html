Comments Data Mining

Jan 15, 2022
Photo by Miguel Á. Padriñán from Pexels

Understanding students' behavior and learning experience is an important task in educational settings. In this project, students are asked to fill out a survey composed of questions related to their learning activities, after each lesson. The students' freely-written comments to each question are helpful to grasp their learning activity. But, the students have to wait a long time before receiving the professor’s feedback to their survey answers. Therefore, the aim of this project is twofold. First, we build prediction models of the students' learning experience based on their comments. Second, we automate the process of giving feedback to the students. Therefore, they don’t need to wait for a long time before receiving the appropriate guidance. This project later evolved into the CourseBot project.
